# Exercises

These are for rereading slowly.

## 1. Points And Vectors

Let:

$$
A =
\begin{bmatrix}
2 \\
1 \\
0
\end{bmatrix},
\quad
B =
\begin{bmatrix}
5 \\
3 \\
1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

Find:

$$
B - A
$$

Ask yourself:

> Is the answer a point or a vector?

## 2. Span

Let:

$$
u =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
0 \\
0
\end{bmatrix},
\quad
v =
\begin{bmatrix}
0 \\
1 \\
0
\end{bmatrix}
$$

What set is reachable by:

$$
au + bv
$$

Can you reach:

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
1 \\
3
\end{bmatrix}
$$

Why or why not?

## 3. Redundant Column

Let:

$$
c_1 =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
0 \\
1
\end{bmatrix},
\quad
c_2 =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
1 \\
2
\end{bmatrix},
\quad
c_3 =
\begin{bmatrix}
0 \\
1 \\
1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

Show:

$$
c_2 = c_1 + c_3
$$

What is the rank of:

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 1 \\
1 & 2 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

## 4. Dot Product

Check whether these are orthogonal:

$$
a =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
1 \\
0
\end{bmatrix},
\quad
b =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
-1 \\
0
\end{bmatrix}
$$

Compute:

$$
a \cdot b
$$

## 5. Projection Onto A Line

Let:

$$
d =
\begin{bmatrix}
4 \\
-2
\end{bmatrix},
\quad
u =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
-1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

Find:

$$
\text{proj}_u(d)
$$

Then compute the leftover:

$$
e = d - \text{proj}_u(d)
$$

Check:

$$
e \cdot u = 0
$$

## 6. Local Square

Let:

$$
A =
\begin{bmatrix}
0 \\
1 \\
0
\end{bmatrix},
\quad
u =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
-1 \\
0
\end{bmatrix},
\quad
v =
\begin{bmatrix}
0 \\
0 \\
1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

Compute the centered square corner:

$$
A + \frac{u}{2} + \frac{v}{2}
$$
